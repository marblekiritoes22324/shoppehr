About Us

Welcome to Shopee HR1! Our system is a dedicated Recruitment Management System for Shopee designed to empower our Human Resources department to efficiently manage job postings, track applicant information, and streamline the entire hiring process from application to onboarding.

Built with modern technology and user experience in mind, it allows our HR staff to post new job vacancies instantly, review and schedule applicants seamlessly, and organize critical recruitment data in one unified, secure, and highly efficient ecosystem.
